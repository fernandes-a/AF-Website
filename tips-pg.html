Here are some simple tips to help plan an eco-friendly holiday

For more tips and information, we recomend checking out Green Global Travel

Avoid flying short distances

You obviously need to fly internationally to reach your destination, but once you arive try travelling by bus or train rather than domestic flights.

Act as you would at home

  • Switch of lights when leaving a room
  • Unplug devices when they're not charging
  • Take short showers

Don't litter

You should also pick up any litter you see.

Try to book non-stop flights

It's the takeoffs and landings that produce the most of an aeroplanes carbon emissions so book non-stop flights whereever possible.

Rent eco-friendly cars

When renting a car consider hybrids or electric vehicles.

Hang up your towels after use

In a hotel, hanging up your towels is the universal sighn that you would like to use it again. This will stop your towels from being washed daily.
